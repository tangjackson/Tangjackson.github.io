Core rule

Practice in interview mode: timer on, AI assistance off, explain tradeoffs out loud, and write post-interview notes immediately.

Target roles

Research Scientist, Member of Technical Staff, ML Engineer, or LLM Systems roles where PyTorch, ML fundamentals, research discussion, and coding all matter.

Weekly rhythm

Five focused study days, one mock day, one lighter review day. Increase company-specific preparation once interviews are scheduled.
